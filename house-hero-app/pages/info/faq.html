Common questions about our services

HouseHero offers a wide range of home services including cleaning, plumbing, electrical work, gardening, handyman services, locksmith services, mechanic services, pest control, air conditioning, and sneaker cleaning.

You can book a service through our website or mobile app. Simply select the service you need, choose your preferred date and time, provide your details, and confirm your booking.

We currently service Midrand and Johannesburg areas. We're expanding to other areas soon, so please check back regularly for updates.

Pricing & Payment

Information about our pricing and payment methods

Our service prices vary depending on the type of service and scope of work. You'll see the exact price when you book a service. We offer competitive rates and transparent pricing with no hidden fees.

We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards, EFT transfers, and cash payments. Payment is typically made after the service is completed to your satisfaction.

Yes, we offer discounts for first-time customers, bulk bookings, and regular customers. We also have seasonal promotions and referral bonuses.

Cancellations made more than 24 hours before the scheduled service are free. Cancellations within 24 hours may incur a small fee to cover our service provider's time.

Service Quality

Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction

If you're not satisfied with our service, please contact us immediately. We'll work to resolve the issue and may offer a refund or free re-service depending on the circumstances.

Yes, we provide warranties on our work. The warranty period varies by service type. For example, plumbing and electrical work typically come with a 30-day warranty.

Safety & Security

Your safety and security are our top priorities

Yes, all our service providers are fully insured. This includes liability insurance to protect against any damage that might occur during the service.

We conduct thorough background checks, verify qualifications and licenses, check references, and perform criminal record checks on all our service providers.

Our service providers follow strict safety protocols, wear proper protective equipment, and are trained in safety procedures. We also have emergency response procedures in place.

Yes, you can track your service provider's location in real-time through our app. You'll receive notifications when they're on their way and when they arrive.

Emergency Services

24/7 emergency support when you need it most

Yes, we offer 24/7 emergency services for urgent situations like plumbing leaks, electrical issues, lockouts, and other critical home problems.

For emergency services, we aim to respond within 30-60 minutes depending on your location and the type of emergency.

Emergencies include situations that pose immediate safety risks or could cause significant damage, such as burst pipes, electrical failures, gas leaks, or lockouts.

Emergency services may have a small additional fee due to the urgent nature and after-hours availability. The exact fee will be clearly communicated before the service.
