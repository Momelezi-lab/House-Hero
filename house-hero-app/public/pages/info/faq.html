Booking Services

How do I book a service?

+

Booking a service is easy! Simply select the service you need from our home page, choose your preferred date and time, fill in your details, and confirm your booking. You'll receive a confirmation email and SMS with all the details.

How far in advance should I book?

+

We recommend booking at least 24 hours in advance for most services. For weekend appointments or during peak seasons, booking 2-3 days ahead is advisable. Emergency services like locksmiths can often be arranged within a few hours.

Can I book multiple services at once?

+

Yes! You can book multiple services in a single session. Simply add each service to your cart and schedule them for the same day or different days. We also offer package deals for multiple services.

What information do I need to provide when booking?

+

You'll need to provide your name, phone number, email address, service address, preferred date and time, and any specific requirements or instructions for the service. For some services, we may also need access information or special instructions.

Pricing & Costs

How are service prices calculated?

+

Our pricing is based on several factors including the type of service, duration, property size, location, and any special requirements. We provide transparent pricing with no hidden fees. You'll see the exact cost before confirming your booking.

Do you offer any discounts or promotions?

+

Yes! We regularly offer promotions including first-time customer discounts, referral bonuses, and seasonal deals. We also offer package discounts when booking multiple services. Check our app or website for current offers.

Are there any additional fees?

+

Our quoted prices include all standard service costs. Additional fees may apply for emergency services outside business hours, travel to remote locations, or if additional materials are required. Any extra costs will be clearly communicated before the service begins.

Can I get a quote before booking?

+

Absolutely! You can get an instant quote by entering your service details on our website or app. For complex services, you can also request a detailed quote by contacting our support team. We're committed to transparent pricing.

Cancellation & Rescheduling

What is your cancellation policy?

+

You can cancel or reschedule your booking free of charge up to 24 hours before the scheduled service time. Cancellations within 24 hours may incur a small fee. Emergency cancellations due to unforeseen circumstances are handled on a case-by-case basis.

How do I cancel or reschedule my booking?

+

You can cancel or reschedule through your account dashboard, by calling our support team, or through the confirmation email/SMS. Simply provide your booking reference number and we'll help you make the changes.

What happens if the service provider cancels?

+

If a service provider needs to cancel, we'll notify you immediately and work to reschedule with another qualified provider. If we can't find a suitable replacement, you'll receive a full refund and we'll help you book for another time.

Safety & Security

How do you ensure the safety of your service providers?

+

All our service providers undergo thorough background checks, identity verification, and skill assessments. They carry identification badges and are tracked during service visits. We also have insurance coverage for all services provided.

What if I'm not satisfied with the service quality?

+

We stand behind the quality of our services. If you're not satisfied, contact us within 24 hours and we'll work to resolve the issue. This may include a re-service, partial refund, or other appropriate compensation.

Are your service providers insured?

+

Yes, all our service providers are fully insured. This includes liability insurance and workers' compensation coverage. Your property and our service providers are protected during all service visits.

What COVID-19 safety measures do you have in place?

+

Our service providers follow strict COVID-19 protocols including wearing masks, maintaining social distance, using hand sanitizer, and disinfecting equipment. They also complete daily health screenings before starting work.

Payment & Billing

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), debit cards, bank transfers, and digital wallets like PayPal and Apple Pay. Payment is processed securely through our platform.

When do I pay for the service?

+

Payment is processed after the service is completed and you've confirmed satisfaction. For some services, a small deposit may be required at booking. You'll receive an invoice within 24 hours of service completion.

Can I get a refund if I'm not satisfied?

+

Yes, we offer a satisfaction guarantee. If you're not completely satisfied with the service, contact us within 24 hours and we'll work to resolve the issue. This may include a full or partial refund depending on the circumstances.

Do you offer payment plans?

+

For larger projects or recurring services, we do offer payment plans. These can be arranged through our support team. Standard services are typically paid in full after completion.

Account & Profile

How do I create an account?

+

Creating an account is simple! Click the "Sign Up" button on our website or app, enter your email address, create a password, and verify your email. You can also sign up using your Google or Facebook account for convenience.

How do I update my profile information?

+

You can update your profile information by going to your account settings. Click on your profile picture or name, then select "Edit Profile" to update your personal information, contact details, and preferences.

Can I have multiple addresses saved?

+

Yes! You can save multiple addresses in your account. This is useful if you need services at different locations like your home, office, or rental property. You can manage these addresses in your account settings.

How do I delete my account?

+

To delete your account, go to your account settings and select "Delete Account." You'll need to confirm your decision and provide a reason. Please note that this action is permanent and cannot be undone.
